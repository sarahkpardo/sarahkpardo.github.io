Some thoughts on neural networks
======

My interest in neural networks was originally inspired by hearing the frequent statement that their behavior is in some way mysterious, but in spite of this they are widely used as tools for analysis, prediction, and decision-making. Immediately I wanted to know: what precisely is their property or component which is mysterious? In what way can it be true to say that 'experts' or 'the people who created them' 'do not fully understand them'? And given that these things are used so extensively, are they mysterious in a way that makes them irresponsible to use, or is it enough to apply them carefully given what we do understand?
